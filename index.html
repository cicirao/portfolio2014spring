About XI

Hi! I'm Xi Rao, an Information Architecture student at Illinois Institute of Technology. I've earned a bachelor's degree on Software Engineering at Huazhong University of Science and Technology.

I'm interested in web design, user experience and photography. I came to Chicago since in 2013, from Wuhan, China, pursuing my dream of becoming a User Experience Designer, which I believe I can make a reality in the United States. It’s hard as well as fun to face the conflicts of inter-cultural communication every day.

I love rock music, doing exercise and cooking Chinese food. I love traveling, and I want to see various sceneries around the world.
